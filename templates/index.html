Boston Dataset Feature Guide

Geography & Demographics

  • CRIM Per capita crime rate by town. Tells us the neighborhood's general safety index.
  • ZN Proportion of residential land zoned for large lots (over 25,000 sq.ft.). High values mean spacious suburban areas.
  • INDUS Proportion of non-retail business acres per town. Measures proximity to industrial zones.
  • CHAS Charles River bounds indicator. 1 if the property tract touches the river, 0 otherwise (premium view!).
  • B Demographic ratio indicator. A statistical measure based on historical Black population density.
  • LSTAT Percentage of lower status population in the area. Strongly correlated with lower overall property prices.

Housing & Infrastructure

  • NOX Nitric oxides concentration (parts per 10 million). Indicates local air quality and traffic congestion.
  • RM Average number of rooms per dwelling. One of the strongest indicators of luxury and size.
  • AGE Proportion of owner-occupied units built prior to 1940. Measures historical and structural age of the neighborhood.
  • DIS Weighted distances to five Boston employment centers. Connects accessibility to jobs.
  • RAD Index of accessibility to radial highways. Indicates travel ease to downtown Boston.
  • TAX Full-value property-tax rate per $10,000. Higher rates fund better local municipal infrastructure.
  • PTRATIO Pupil-teacher ratio by town. Reflects quality of local schooling and school resources.
